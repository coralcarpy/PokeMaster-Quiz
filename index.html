About PokéMaster Quiz
A fan-made Pokémon quiz platform

PokéMaster Quiz is a free, browser-based Pokémon trivia game featuring multiple game modes designed to test and improve your Pokémon knowledge.

Game Modes

  • Daily Challenge — One challenge per day, same for everyone
  • Silhouette Challenge — Identify Pokémon from their shadow
  • Cry Challenge — Identify Pokémon from their audio cry
  • Type Arena — Master type effectiveness matchups
  • Pokémon Wordle — Guess the Pokémon in 8 tries

Data & Privacy

All Pokémon data is fetched from PokéAPI (pokeapi.co), a free and open RESTful Pokémon API. No personal data is collected. Your game progress is stored only in your browser's localStorage.
